The amber column is the plain-English pain and trigger for leadership; the shaded columns are the constant AI spine. v1 draft: technical mappings are at the control-family / criteria level; exact sub-control IDs are validated per engagement. Two domains, human oversight and fairness (rows 5 and 8), sit outside every security regime, not SOC 2, ISO 27001, CMMC, or 800-171. They exist only in the AI spine. That gap is the case for AI governance as its own discipline.
